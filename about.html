BRAND STORY

Austin Plywood was rewarded by customer preference for its outstanding quality; it became the only brand in the country to be approved by Lloyds Register and was certified as a BS-1088 product

Founded by father son duo of Mr. Surendra Agarwal and Mr. Nishant Agarwal in the year 2006, Austin Plywood started with the objective of fulfilling the needs of the discerning user for high quality premium plywood.

The market was flush with popular brands who were manufacturing and supplying ordinary quality plywood in the name of water proof category. Austin Plywood changed the landscape and was immediately rewarded by customer preference for its outstanding quality, it became the only brand in the country to be approved by Lloyds Register and was certified as a BS-1088 product.
